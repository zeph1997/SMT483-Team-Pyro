Introduction Last updated: 2022-04-10

Welcome to Pyro Fire Alarm Management System.

This page serves as a guide to the features and functions available in this system.

There are 3 main pages in the system:
Page Description
Dashboard Map overview of all Facilities' Fire Status
Camera Management Summary of all Facilities' Fire Camera details
Camera Logs Historical logging records of all Facilities' Fire Camera details

Note

Navigate to any of these pages through the side menu bar. Collapse side-menu bar for fuller view.

Dashboard Guide

The "Dashboard" page displays the live status of all Facilities' fire cameras' status.

The Live Dashboard has 3 main parts:
  1. Map Dashboard (bottom left)
  2. Summary Cards (bottom right)
  3. Alert Banners (top)

Map Dashboard

The Map Dashboard displays all cameras' status through markers mapped to their respective locations.

Possible Markers:
Marker Status Description
Fire Located
Fire has been detected from both Thermal Camera and Camera Vision in this camera location
Potential Fire Located
Fire has been detected from either the Thermal Camera, or the Camera Vision in this camera location
No Fire
No fire is detected from both the Thermal Camera and the Camera Vision in this camera location
False Alarm
A fire was detected from one/both thermal and vision cameras, but was muted.
Fire Department Dispatched
SCDF has been dispatched to this camera location as a potential fire was detected.
Available Firestation
Firestation dispatched to the detected fire site.
Marker Functions:

You may trigger actions depending on the marker type

Marker(s) Feature/Function
Dispatch Fire Station
Dispatch a team to a potential Fire Site
Clear Dispatch Fire Station
Recall a team to a potential Fire Site
Mute Fire
If you are definite the detected fire was a false alarm, you may mute the marker by clicking the marker, and clicking "mute"
Ummute
Unmute if you accidentally muted an actual fire
Available/Dispatched Firestations
Click on the marker to get more details on the Firestation

Tip

Click on the marker to read more details and trigger actions!

Summary Cards

The summary cards gives you a quick overview on the fire statuses of all Facilities. The count of each Camera status is summed up.

Alert Banners

Alert Banners are triggered banners to warn you of potential fires detected. If no fires are detected, the banners will not be visible.

Possible Alert Banners:
Scenario Alert Banner
≥1 markers are orange
(i.e potential fire warning, detected from either thermal/vision cameras)
Potential Fire warnings located!
≥1 markers are red
(i.e fire has been detected from both thermal and vision cameras)
Fire warnings located!
Markers are all green / grey
(i.e no fire/false alarms detected from both thermal and vision cameras)		 None

Visualisation Tips

You may follow these tips to avoid over-cluttering the map dashboard, and focus on what you are most interested in

Tip 1: Change Map Layer

The view of map layers can be changed (Satellite/ Street/ Grey) accordingly to suit your needs

Tip 2: Filter Markers

Various markers can be filtered accordingly to your focus

Tip 3: Zooming Layers

You may zoom in/out the Map Dashboard using the +/- button at the top left, or by pinching your mouse pad
